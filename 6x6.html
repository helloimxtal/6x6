Schulte-Table.com - is online game, that contains most powerful tools to upgrade your brain abilities based on Schulte table, and other methods to train your brain.
While training, you have to be relaxed and concentrated!
Focus on the center of the table, try to see whole table while playing and try also not to move your eyes while searching numbers and clicking on them.
Click on boxes from 1 to 36 (or in reversed order) as fast as you can. In Reaction mode click on red box.
For even better results - you have to treat this game as daily meditation practice.
I also recommend you while playing listen to special music for meditation, you can listen to radio for meditation right here. (click play button in buttom left corner)
After training each day:
  • soon you will find out that you can concentrate on things very fast;
  • after months - you will be able to find and analyze information much faster;
  • after 1 year of training you will be surprised, how everything comes easily to you.

    For example:
  • If you are googling something you don’t just stare at your screen, you type and click, find and click, and after working out here you would be able to do it much faster.
  • If you are programmer, you will be able to see more of your code at once, find functions, procedures, classes, needed information in documentation much faster and be more productive.
  • Even if you are gamer, this training will help you to be faster, see sharp more area of the screen, you will get more frags and headshots :).

    How to train: in the left buttom corner you will find your daily progress, every day minimum is to make it 100%.
    if you want to make progress in your training you have to play all modes of the game: Reaction, Classic Light, Classic Light Reverse, Classic Original, Classic Original Reverse and Memory.
    also after playing month or more, you can try to beat your previous month's score(see chart below score tables): score & chart
    daily progress is counting so:
    if you are beginner and you don’t have results in result-table on the right of screen, then first 2 lessons you have to play 5 times all modes:
  • Reaction
  • Classic Light
  • Classic Light Reverse
  • Classic Original
  • Classic Original Reverse
  • Memory
    (each must take less than 60 sec, but if 60 sec isn't enough for you than don't worry, you soon will be able to solve it much faster), so on the beginning of third lesson you will have 10 lines on each of your result table.
  • now you try to play until you will score top 10 2 times - this till you will get total 40 lines of play
  • now you try to play until you will score top 10 1 times till 100 lines of play
  • after you try play till you will score top 25 1 time each game - this till you will get 140 lines of play
  • after you try play till you will score top 50 1 time each game - this till you will get total 300 lines of play
  • after that and till end you will have to score top 100 each game
